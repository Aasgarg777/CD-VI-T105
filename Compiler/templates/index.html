üî§ What is Lexing?

Lexing, or Lexical Analysis, is the process of converting a sentence into tokens with part-of-speech (POS) tags. Each word is labeled as a noun, verb, determiner, adjective, etc.

üå≥ What is Parsing?

Parsing takes those tokens and builds a grammatical structure (AST). It helps identify how components like subject, verb, and object relate to each other.
